🤠 About Me

Ying Wang, (Graduate Student Member, IEEE), completed his undergraduate studies at Henan University of Engineering, majoring in Communication Engineering. In 2021, he passed the Master's Entrance Examination of Harbin Engineering University and joined the Xinghai Academic Team of "Communication Systems and Technologies in Complex Electromagnetic Environments". In 2022, he applied for a PhD degree, majoring in Information and Communication Engineering.

His main research interests include multicarrier wireless communications (FBMC, OFDM, etc.), millimeter-wave MIMO communications, and antenna arrays, etc. In addition, he is also interested in interdisciplinary technologies such as electromagnetic information theory.

王英，本科就读于河南工程学院，通信工程专业。在2021年，通过了哈尔滨工程大学的硕士研究生入学考试，并加入“复杂电磁环境下通信系统与技术”学术团队学习深造。在2022年，成功申请攻读哈尔滨工程大学，信息与通信工程专业的博士学位。主要研究方向包括多载波无线通信，毫米波MIMO通信，以及天线阵列等。特别地，感兴趣的研究方向还包括电磁信息论等跨学科技术。
